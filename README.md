# Our Citibike Story:

## Where do rides start (and end)?
* map of start/endpoint

## Who rides?
* by usertype
* by gender 
* by age

## When do they ride?
* by day of the week
* by hour of the day



## How long is a ride?
* by usertype
* by day of the week (weekend vs. weekday)
* by gender
* by age


## What bikes are ridden the most (might need maintenance)?
* sum total of hours ridden (for the given time period)
